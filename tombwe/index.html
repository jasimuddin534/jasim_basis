OUR COMPANY

TPL is a Zambian privatisation success which handles the majority of the Zambia Tobacco crop.

Tombwe Processing Limited (TPL) is a dynamic organisation which processes tobacco for a range of international merchants. We realise the importance of continuous improvements and therefore strive to improve our processing and storage capacity to meet the increasing international demand of Zambian grown Tobacco.

TPL with its business partner, Contraf - Nicotex - Tobacco GmbH (CNT), contracts small producers and commercial farmers for the production of tobacco. Financial support and Agronomy advice are part of the services delivered. TPL is actively involved in improving the sustainability of tobacco production and the elimination of child labour in the tobacco industry. Through CNT the company has a presence on the ECLT board (Elimination of Child Labour in Tobacco).

OUR FACTORY

Never stop improving is our factory slogan.This is becoming apparent through the continuous improvements to our processing operations. The improvements will guarantee the high quality tobacco grown in Zambia will meet the stringent international processing standards set by cigarette manufacturers world-wide. Our experienced and dedicated processing team is supported by our Quality Control department to ensure total customer satisfaction at all times.

OUR AGRONOMY DEPARTMENT (Farmers Support)

We support our farmer both financially and technically. We have a team of experienced extension workers and work closely with exemplary farm input organisations.

Tobacco growing is a significant part of our company values. It is an area with great social, environmental and economic impact. TPL has a direct relationship with commercial farmers, as well as with over 2000 small growers via project coordinators and a strong extension team to implement Good Agricultural Practices (GAP).

TPL over the years has developed a bespoke program, to support farmers with:

Crop Finance

  • Seed
  • Fertiliser
  • Approved Chemicals

Tree Planting

Supply of Packaging Materials

Training at district level and farm visits

Capital Loans for eg. Rocket Barns

Transport from Farm to Floor

TPL has started several initiatives to work on reforestation within the tobacco producing areas. In future TPL foresees farmers to be self-sufficient in wood for curing the tobacco.

TPL works with its contracted farmers on eradication of child labour on their farms in line with ECLT (Eradicate Child Labour in Tobacco) regulations. TPL also works at creating safe youth employment on farms in line with International Labour Organisation (ILO) standards.
